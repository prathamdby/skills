# Phrases to Remove

## Discourse Markers

AI overuses signposting to simulate structure. These words announce transitions the reader can already feel.

- furthermore
- moreover
- importantly
- crucially
- notably
- interestingly
- it is worth noting
- at its core
- in essence
- fundamentally
- inherently

## Throat-Clearing Openers

These phrases stall before the point. Cut them and start with the point.

- here's the thing
- here's what
- here's why
- the truth is
- let me be clear
- it turns out
- the real issue is
- the uncomfortable truth
- I'll say it again
- can we talk about

## Permission-Granting

AI inserts false reassurance where none is needed.

- and that's okay
- make no mistake
- here's why that matters
- this is important because
- let that sink in

## Hedges and Adverbs

All `-ly` words. Also these common softeners and intensifiers.

- really
- just
- simply
- actually
- genuinely
- literally
- honestly
- truly
- deeply
- inevitably
- interestingly
- importantly

## Filler Phrases

- at its core
- in today's world
- it's worth noting
- at the end of the day
- when it comes to
- in a world where
- the reality is

## Lazy Extremes

Sweeping claims that substitute for specifics.

- every
- always
- never
- everyone
- everybody
- nobody
- everything
- all

## Vague Declaratives

Sentences that announce importance without showing the specific thing.

- the implications are significant
- the reasons are structural
- this is the deepest problem
- the stakes are high
- the consequences are real
